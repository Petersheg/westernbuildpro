Who We Are

Western BuildPro Infrastructure Development, a private limited company with its HQ in Lagos, Nigeria was founded in 2015, The company sets itself out to be a leading firm in the construction and real estate industries in Nigeria.

We provide value added services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them by either tailoring the structures to their needs or anticipating their needs and exceeding their expectations. We try to gain the trust of our clients through exceptional performance by every member of the team.

OUR MISSION

At western buildPro, every person is a professional in their field and core competence, as well as a member of the team and a team player, with expectations that they carry out their duties and perform their roles to their fullest capacities and potential.

Our objective is to establish a long term relationship without our clients based on design, quality structures, safety and an anticipation of their needs, ensuring cost effectiveness in all of these. To help achieve this mission, all members of our team are treated fairly and are constantly involved in projects and improvement processes that allows them to properly utilize their skill set.
